## 🌎 About Groumeet

**Groumeet** is a free online platform where anyone can instantly meet new people through **live chat rooms**, **private messaging**, and **cam-to-cam video chat**.

No registration is required. Simply choose a nickname, select your gender, and start chatting with people from all around the world.

Whether you're looking to make friends, have interesting conversations, or join active communities, Groumeet makes connecting simple and fast.
